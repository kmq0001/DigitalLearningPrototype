Digital Logic: Learning Binary Circuits & Equations

Welcome to Learning Digital Logic! Ever wondered how memory space works? Or even a simple light switch? Well, digital logic is a good way to start! We aim to provide you with an easy introduction to binary logic, circuits and equations. Start with our tutorial videos, and when you feel confident enough move on to some fun circuit and equation simplification challenges! Or if you really want to challenge yourself, why not go solo on the circuits free style page?

Learning

Here you will find videos learning the basics of binary logic and how it translates to circuitry. Also find the simplification of binary equations. If you get really stuck, you can always use the handy cheat sheet.
